BE SUSPICIOUS

Phishing emails are sent with the intention of tricking the user into clicking on a link that leads to a malicious website. If you do this, your personal information or device could be compromised. How to detect it?

To view the URL for the link, as shown in the screenshot, move your cursor pointer over it and hold it there. Do not click on the link if the URL does not correspond to the link text or if something appears off about it.
