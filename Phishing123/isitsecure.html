IS It Secure

Check the web address that is displayed in the URL bar at the top of your browser. Websites that are encrypted begin with https rather than http. The letter s at the end signifies that the website uses encryption.

Check to see if there is a padlock icon, if there is a padlock icon in the address bard or next to the URL, then you know that the website is secure. And to view the website’s security certificate click on padlock.

Make sure to verify the authenticity of the security certificate, to inspect the website’s security certificate, you will need to click on the lock icon. Verify that the certificate was issued by a trusted organization.

Look for additional signs of protection: A green address bar or a shield icon are two examples of additional security indications that may be displayed by a certain web browsers.
